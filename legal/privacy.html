Privacy Policy for MemoryVerse

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MemoryVerse ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register for MemoryVerse, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile avatar selection

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we automatically collect:

  • Bible verses you've read, practiced, and memorized
  • Practice session data (dates, scores, progress)
  • Streak and achievement data
  • XP and level progress
  • App usage analytics (screens visited, features used)
  • Device information (OS version, device model for compatibility)

2.3 AI Feature Data (Premium Users)

If you use our AI-powered prayer features:

  • Text you submit for prayer generation (processed but not permanently stored)
  • Generated prayer responses
  • Usage count for rate limiting

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our Bible memorization service
  • Track your learning progress and streaks
  • Generate personalized AI prayers (premium feature)
  • Send you important updates about your account
  • Improve our app based on usage patterns
  • Process premium subscriptions through RevenueCat
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only with the following trusted third parties:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase - Database and authentication (encrypted storage)
  • RevenueCat - Payment processing for premium subscriptions
  • Perplexity AI - AI prayer generation (text is processed but not stored by Perplexity)
  • Firebase Analytics - Anonymous usage analytics

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4.3 What We DO NOT Do

  • We DO NOT sell your personal information
  • We DO NOT share your prayer submissions publicly
  • We DO NOT use your data for advertising
  • We DO NOT track you across other apps or websites

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage via Supabase
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies to protect user data
  • Content moderation to prevent inappropriate AI-generated content
  • Regular security audits

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Practice history: Retained for progress tracking
  • AI prayer inputs: Processed in real-time, not permanently stored
  • Analytics: Anonymized and aggregated data retained indefinitely

7. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of optional data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pintayo.com

8. Children's Privacy

MemoryVerse is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. However:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent
  • If you believe a child under 13 has provided us data, contact us immediately
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with GDPR.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: support@pintayo.com
  • App: MemoryVerse - Settings → Privacy & Support

12. App Store Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • Google Play Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
